SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
NOVEMBER 23, 2021
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Tuesday, November 23, 2021,
at 3:02 pm, in the Council Chambers, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of engaging Council in a
workshop to discuss Vancouver Plan and hear from relevant experts. This meeting was convened by
electronic means as authorized under Section 566 of the Vancouver Charter.

WELCOME
The Deputy Mayor acknowledged we are on the unceded territories of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh Nations and we thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to working
with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Deputy Mayor also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s staff who work
hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and play.

Special Council Meeting
Minutes, Tuesday, November 23, 2021 2
1. Vancouver Plan - Emerging Directions and Big Ideas: A Dialogue with National and
International City Builders
Andrew Pask, Planner, Vancouver Plan, provided a brief overview and process update on the
Vancouver Plan, as well as a summary of public engagement.
Subsequently, Alex Bozikovic, Architecture & Planning Critic, Globe and Mail and Facilitator for the
meeting, presented brief opening remarks, as well as an overview of the workshop. Alex then
introduced the panelists, who then each provided a presentation related to their area of expertise.
• Paty Rios, Executive Director, New Cities Foundation
• Andre Brumfield, Design Director, Gensler Cities & Urban Design
• Solomon Wong, President and CEO, interVISTAS
Following the panelist presentations, the panel members responded to questions from Council which
was facilitated by Alex.
Following questions and discussions, the panelists provided closing remarks.
