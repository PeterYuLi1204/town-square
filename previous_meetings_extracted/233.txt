MAYOR’S BUDGET TASK FORCE IMPLEMENTATION
OVERSIGHT COMMITTEE
MEETING MINUTES
MAY 21, 2024
A Meeting of the Mayor’s Budget Task Force Implementation Oversight Committee was held on
Tuesday, May 21, 2024, at 1:00 PM, in the Mayor’s Ceremonial Boardroom, Third Floor, City
Hall.
PRESENT: Councillor Zhou (Chair)
Councillor Dominato
Councillor Meizner
CITY MANAGER’S OFFICE: Paul Mochrie, City Manager
ALSO PRESENT: Trevor Ford, Mayor’s Chief of Staff
Mellisa Morphy, Mayor’s Deputy Chief of Staff
REPORTS
1. Chair Selection
MOVED by Councillor Dominato
SECONDED by Councillor Meiszner
THAT Councillor Zhou be appointed Chair of the Mayor’s Budget Task Force
Implementation Oversight Committee.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY
2. Prioritization of Task Force Recommendations
The City Manager, along with Mayor’s Office staff, responded to questions and comments on
potential short-term, medium-term, and long-term goals for Mayor’s Budget Task Force
recommendations.
3. Public Communications
The City Manager, along with Mayor’s Office staff, responded to questions and comments on a
“one-stop” webpage for publicly disseminating progress on Mayor’s Budget Task Force
recommendations.

Mayor’s Budget Task Force Implementation Oversight Committee Meeting
Minutes, May 21, 2024 2
4. Next Steps
The Committee agreed, by consensus, that the Mayor’s Office will draft terms of reference for a
Finance Committee of Council, and the City Manager’s Office will prepare a presentation on the
current status of Task Force recommendations.
ADJOURNMENT
The Committee agreed, by consensus, to adjourn the meeting.
The Committee adjourned at 2:00 PM.
