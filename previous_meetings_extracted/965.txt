SPECIAL COUNCIL
COURT OF REVISION – BUSINESS IMPROVEMENT AREAS
MEETING MINUTES
FEBRUARY 26, 2019
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Tuesday,
February 26, 2019, at 6:02 pm, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of
holding a Court of Revision in order to hear from assessed property owners regarding errors in
the names of assessed owners in Business Improvement Areas, or the measurements of the
property, including the frontage or any other property measurements.

1. DELEGATIONS CONCERNING ERRORS
The Court of Revision had before it special assessment notification letters that were sent
Thursday, November 22, 2018, to the assessed property owners in the following Business
Improvement Areas:
a) Fraser Street Business Improvement Area
b) Gastown Business Improvement Area
c) Proposed Expansion of Hastings North Business Improvement Area
d) Marpole Business Improvement Area
e) Point Grey Village Business Improvement Area
f) South Granville Business Improvement Area
g) Victoria Drive Business Improvement Area
h) Yaletown Business Improvement Area

Court of Revision
Minutes, Tuesday, February 26, 2019 2
Staff from the Business Improvement Area Program advised that no correspondence had been
received regarding errors or omissions in the names of assessed property owners, or the length
of the frontage or other measurements of the parcels.
The Mayor called for speakers relating only to the above-noted errors or omissions, and none
were present.
