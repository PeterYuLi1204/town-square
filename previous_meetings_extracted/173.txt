SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
November 5, 2024
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Tuesday, November 5,
2024, at 4 pm, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of considering a
leave of absence for Councillor Boyle. This Special Council meeting was convened in person
and via electronic means as authorized by Part 14 of the Procedure By-law.

WELCOME
The Acting Mayor acknowledged we are on the unceded homelands of the Musqueam,
Squamish, and Tsleil-Waututh People. We thank them for having cared for this land and look
forward to working with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
1. Request for Leave of Absence
MOVED by Councillor Dominato
SECONDED by Councillor Klassen
THAT Councillor Boyle be granted an unpaid Leave of Absence for personal reasons
from all Council related business including meetings on November 5, 2024, to
December 12, 2024.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY (Vote No. 10293)
