SPECIAL COUNCIL
COURT OF REVISION – LOCAL IMPROVEMENTS
MEETING MINUTES
NOVEMBER 22, 2018
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Thursday,
November 22, 2018, at 5:01 pm, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for
the purpose of holding a Court of Revision pertaining to local improvement projects.

RESOLVE INTO COURT OF REVISION
MOVED by Councillor Wiebe
SECONDED by Councillor Dominato
THAT this Council resolve itself into Court of Revision for the purpose of hearing
complaints against the proposed assessments or the accuracy of frontage
measurements, or any other complaint which is by law cognizable by the Court, Deputy
Mayor Carr in the Chair.
