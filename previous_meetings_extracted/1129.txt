SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
AUGUST 4, 2017
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Friday, August 4, 2017,
at 2:31 pm, in Committee Room 1, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of apponting a Chief
Election Officer for the conduct of a by-election to fill nine school trustee vacancies for
School District No. 39.
