REPORT TO COUNCIL
COUNCIL COVID-19 RECOVERY COMMITTEE
JUNE 17, 2020
A meeting of the Council COVID-19 Recovery Committee on was held on Wednesday,
June 17, 2020, at 1:06 pm in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall. This Council meeting
was convened by electronic means as authorized under the Order of the Minister of Public
Safety and Solicitor General of the Province of British Columbia - the Emergency Program Act,
Ministerial Order No. M139. (Note: on June 17, 2020, M139 was replaced with M192)

WELCOME
The Chair acknowledged we are on the unceded territories of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh Nations and we thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to
working with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Chair also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s staff who work
hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and play.
COMMITTEE OF THE WHOLE
MOVED by Councillor Carr
SECONDED by Councillor Dominato
THAT this Council resolve itself into Committee of the Whole, Councillor De Genova in
the Chair, to consider the agenda items.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY

(Councillors Boyle, Bligh and Mayor Stewart absent for the vote)
b) CCRC Liaisons:
Councillor Carr; and
Councillor Dominato
Councillors Carr and Dominato provided an update and, along with the City Manager,
responded to questions.

next Committee meeting on July 3, 2020.
