SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING
COURT OF REVISION – BUSINESS IMPROVEMENT AREAS
MINUTES
FEBRUARY 2, 2023
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Thursday,
February 2, 2023, at 3:16 pm, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of
hearing from assessed property owners regarding errors in the names of the owners of the
property and the measurements of the property, including the frontage or any other property
measurement, and any variation applied. This Special Council meeting was convened by
electronic means as authorized in Part 14 of the Procedure By-law.

1. DELEGATIONS CONCERNING ERRORS
The Court of Revision had before it special assessment notification letters that were sent on or
before Tuesday, November 15, 2022, to the assessed property owners within the following
Business Improvement Areas (BIAs):
a) Dunbar Village Business Improvement Area (BIA renewal)
b) Robson Street Business Improvement Area (BIA renewal)
Staff from the BIA Program advised that one piece of correspondence related to the Robson
Street Business Improvement Area had been received regarding an error to the name of an
assessed owner. Staff informed Council that the error was due to a change in ownership after a

recent property transfer. The roll used for the notifications listed the former owner, but the roll
had already been updated to show the new owner by the time the complaint had been received.
The error was rectified by mailing the special assessment notification letter to the correct owner
using the updated property assessment roll.
The Chair called for speakers relating only to the above-noted errors and none were present.
