December 3, 2019, at 9:33 am, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose
of hearing from speakers on the 2020 Capital and Operating Budget.

WELCOME
The Mayor acknowledged we are on the unceded territories of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh Nations and we thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to
working with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Mayor also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s staff who work
hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and play.

Special Council Meeting
Minutes, Tuesday, December 3, 2019 2
1. 2020 Capital and Operating Budget
The following members of the City’s Leadership Team, along with the Director of City-wide
Financial Planning and Analysis, presented highlights on the 2020 Capital and Operating
Budget:
• Patrice Impey, General Manager, Finance, Risk & Supply Chain Management/CFO
• Cheryl Nelms, Acting General Manager, Engineering Services
• Sandra Singh, General Manager, Arts, Culture and Community Services
• Malcolm Bromley, General Manager, Parks & Recreation
• Gil Kelley, General Manager, Planning, Urban Design & Sustainability
• Darrell Reid, Fire Chief, Vancouver Fire Rescue Services (VFRS)
• Adam Palmer, Chief Constable, Vancouver Police Department (VPD)
• Christina de Castell, Chief Librarian, Vancouver Public Library
• Nick Kassam, General Manager, Real Estate and Facilities Management
Council heard from 38 speakers on the 2020 Capital and Operating Budget; twelve who spoke
either in full or general support with suggestions and 26 who spoke in opposition; concerns raised
included the proposed increases to property taxes and the VPD budget.
* * * * *
During the hearing of speakers Council recessed at 11:58 am and reconvened at 1:06 pm;
and recessed at 5:05 pm and reconvened at 6:05 pm
* * * * *
Following the hearing of speakers, the City Manager, along with members of the City’s
Leadership Team and the Managing Director of Homelessness Services & Affordable Housing
Programs, responded to questions.
Note: discussion and decision on the 2020 Capital and Operating Budget is scheduled for the
Council meeting on December 10, 2019.
