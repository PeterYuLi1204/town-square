COURT OF REVISION
BUSINESS IMPROVEMENT AREAS
MINUTES
FEBRUARY 23, 2021
A Court of Revision of the City of Vancouver was held on Tuesday, February 23, 2021, at
4:04 pm, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of hearing from
assessed property owners regarding errors in the names of the owners of the property and the
measurements of the property, including the frontage or any other property measurement, and
any variation applied.

The Chair acknowledged we are on the unceded territories of the Musqueam, Squamish, and

working with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
1. DELEGATIONS CONCERNING ERRORS
The Court of Revision had before it special assessment notification letters that were sent on or
before Wednesday, December 9, 2020, to the assessed property owners within the following
Business Improvement Areas (BIAs):
a) Hastings Crossing Business Improvement Area (BIA renewal)
b) Kerrisdale Business Improvement Area (BIA renewal)
c) Kitsilano 4th Avenue Business Improvement Area (BIA renewal)
d) Mount Pleasant Business Improvement Area (BIA renewal)
e) West Broadway Business Improvement Area (BIA renewal)

f) West End Business Improvement Area (BIA renewal)
g) Hastings North Business Improvement Area (proposed expansion)
Staff from the BIA Program advised that one correspondence had been received regarding error
to the name of the assessed owner or to the length of the frontage or other measurements of
the parcel. Staff reported that no further action is required as the transfer of ownership for the
above-noted property is reflected in the 2021 property tax roll.
The Chair called for speakers relating only to the above-noted errors and none were present.
