COURT OF REVISION
BUSINESS IMPROVEMENT AREAS
MINUTES
FEBRUARY 11, 2020
A Court of Revision of the City of Vancouver was held on Tuesday, February 11, 2020, at
6:05 pm, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of hearing from
assessed property owners regarding errors in the names of the owners of the property and the
measurements of the property, including the frontage or any other property measurement, and
any variation applied.

WELCOME
The Chair acknowledged we are on the unceded territories of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh Nations and we thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to
working with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Chair also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s staff who work
hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and play.
1. DELEGATIONS CONCERNING ERRORS
The Court of Revision had before it special assessment notification letters that were sent
Wednesday, November 27, 2019, to the assessed property owners within the following
Business Improvement Areas (BIAs):
a) Downtown Vancouver Business Improvement Area (BIA renewal)
b) Mount Pleasant Business Improvement Area (proposed expansion)

Court of Revision – Business Improvement Areas
Minutes, Tuesday, February 11, 2020 2
Staff from the BIA Program advised that no correspondence had been received regarding errors
to the names of the assessed owners or to the length of the frontage or other measurements of
the parcel.
The Chair called for speakers relating only to the above-noted errors and none were present.
