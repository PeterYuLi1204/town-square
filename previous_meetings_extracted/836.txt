SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
March 23, 2020
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on
Monday, March 23, 2020, at 10:09 am, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the
purpose of amending the Emergency By-law No. 12661 and the Ticket Offences By-law
No. 9360 to include enforcement authority and possible fines and to convene a meeting closed
to the public later in the week. This Special Council meeting was convened by electronic means
as authorized by Part 14 of the Procedure By-law and the Vancouver Charter.

WELCOME
The Mayor acknowledged we are on the unceded territories of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh Nations and we thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to
working with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Mayor also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s staff who work
hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and play.
