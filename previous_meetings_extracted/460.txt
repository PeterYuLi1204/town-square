REPORT TO COUNCIL
AUDITOR GENERAL COMMITTEE
JULY 15, 2022
A meeting of the Auditor General Committee was held on Friday, July 15, 2022, at 1:03 pm, in
the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall. This Committee meeting was convened by
electronic means as authorized under Part 14 of the Procedure By-law.

WELCOME
The Chair acknowledged we are on the unceded homelands of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh People and we thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to
working with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Chair also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s staff who work
hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and play.
1. Whistleblowing – Reporting, Investigation and Protection ADMIN-002
The Auditor General provided an overview of the Report, and along with the Chief of Human
Resources, responded to questions.
Following questions, it was,

Auditor General Committee
Minutes, Friday, July 15, 2022 2
MOVED by Councillor Hardwick
THAT the Committee recommend to Council
THAT the Report entitled “Whistleblowing – Reporting, Investigation and Protection
ADMIN-002”, dated June 30, 2022, be received for information.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY (Vote No. 08680)
2. Amendments to City of Vancouver Policies
The Auditor General provided a brief introduction to the item.
MOVED by Councillor Kirby-Yung
THAT the Committee recommend to Council
THAT the Auditor General Committee endorse the Auditor General’s recommendations
contained in Appendix A of the Report entitled “Amendments to City of Vancouver
Policies”, dated July 7, 2022, and recommend the policy changes to Council for
approval.

3. Office of the Auditor General Status Update
The Auditor General presented an update on the status of the Office of the Auditor General and
responded to questions.
ADJOURNMENT
MOVED by Councillor Hardwick
THAT the meeting be adjourned.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY
