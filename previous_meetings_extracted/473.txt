SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
June 29, 2022
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Wednesday, June 29,
2022, at 9:34 am, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of considering
the 2023-2026 Capital Plan: Final Plan. This Special Council meeting was convened by
electronic means as authorized in Part 14 of the Procedure By-law.

WELCOME
The Mayor acknowledged we are on the unceded homelands of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh People. We thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to working
with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Mayor also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s staff who work
hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and play.
1. 2023-2026 Capital Plan: Final Plan
The City Manager introduced the item and staff from Finance, Risk and Supply Chain
Management, provided a presentation. Subsequently the aforementioned staff along with staff
from Arts, Cultural and Community Services; Engineering Services; Planning, Urban Design,
and Sustainability; Parks and Recreation; and Vancouver Fire and Rescue Services, responded
to questions.

THAT the following be added as D:
THAT Council direct staff to vigorously explore funding opportunities from a

accelerate energy retrofits, particularly of city-owned buildings including

assess including solar voltaic and solar thermal capacity into retrofit projects in
order to reduce costs of heating water and to provide resilience in light of B.C.
Hydro’s projected lack of capacity to meet electricity demands beyond 2030.
