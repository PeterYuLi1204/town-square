SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
(BUSINESS LICENCE HEARING)
MARCH 5, 2025
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Wednesday,
March 5, 2025, at 9:30 am, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of
holding a Business Licence Hearing to consider the decision of the Chief Licence Inspector to
suspend the 2024 business licence (“Business Licence”) issued to Sukhvinder Sandhu (the
Licencee”) for a short term rental accommodation at 2303-128 W Cordova Street (the
“Premises”). This Special Council meeting was convened in person and via electronic means as
authorized by Part 14 of the Procedure By-law and the Vancouver Charter.
PRESENT: Councillor Peter Meiszner, Chair
Councillor Brian Montague

CITY CLERK’S OFFICE: Connie Law, Meeting Coordinator

Iain Dixon, Assistant Director - Regulatory Litigation, Legal Services, was present on behalf of
the City of Vancouver. Sukhvinder Sandhu, the Licencee, was present on their own behalf.
Iain Dixon provided opening remarks and reviewed relevant legislation as set out in section 275
of the Vancouver Charter and section 25.1(3) of Licence By-law No. 4450.
In support of the allegations set out in the evidence brief, Iain Dixon called Sarah Hicks, Chief
Licence Inspector, as a witness for the City of Vancouver.
Iain Dixon along with Panel members directed questions to the witness.
The Licencee also directed questions to the witness.
The Licencee provided opening remarks and responded to the allegations submitted by the
witness.
The Licencee did not have witnesses.
Panel members directed questions to the Licencee.
The Licencee responded to questions from the Panel.
Iain Dixon directed questions to the witness for the City of Vancouver.
The Licencee provided closing submissions.

PANEL MEMBERS DISCUSSION
MOVED by Councillor Montague
SECONDED by Councillor Klassen
THAT the Business Licence Hearing Panel uphold the decision of the Chief Licence
Inspector to suspend the 2024 Business Licence No. 24-159924 for a Short Term Rental
(“Business Licence”) issued to Sukhvinder Sandhu (the “Licencee”) for the premises
located at 2303-128 W Cordova Street, Vancouver, BC, on the basis that the
requirement of principle residency has not been proven to the satisfaction of this Panel.
CARRIED UNAIMOUSLY (Vote No. 10557)
ADJOURNMENT
MOVED by Councillor Montague
SECONDED by Councillor Klassen
THAT the meeting be adjourned.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY
The Business Licence Hearing Panel adjourned at 10:19 am.
