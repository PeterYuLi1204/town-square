WELCOME
The Chair acknowledged we are on the unceded homelands of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh People. We thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to working
with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Chair also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s staff who work
hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and play.

1. June 2025 Follow-Up Report
The Auditor General provided a presentation and, along with staff from Arts, Culture and
Community Services and the Vancouver Police Department, responded to questions.
MOVED by Councillor Kirby-Yung
SECONDED by Councillor Klassen
THAT the Committee recommend to Council
THAT the Auditor General Committee request the Chair to send a letter to the
Vancouver Police Board requesting them to fulfil prior requests to provide a
written response to the Committee detailing if the Vancouver Police Department
secured external cybersecurity expertise.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY (Vote No. 10818)
(Councillor Zhou absent for the vote)
