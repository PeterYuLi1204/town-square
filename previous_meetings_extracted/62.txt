SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
(BUSINESS LICENCE HEARING)
JULY 16, 2025
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Wednesday, July 16,
2025, at 9:32am, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of holding a
Business Licence Hearing to determine whether to deny, grant, or grant with conditions the
application made by Michael Enriquez (the “Applicant”) for a 2025 business licence (“Business
Licence”) for a short-term rental accommodation at 2606-1068 Hornby Street. This Special
Council meeting was convened in person and via electronic means as authorized by Part 14 of
the Procedure By-law and the Vancouver Charter.

Iain Dixon, Assistant Director - Regulatory Litigation, Legal Services, was present on behalf of
the City of Vancouver. Michael Enriquez, the Applicant, was present and accompanied by Dan
Barber, Lawyer, Singleton Urquhart Reynolds Vogue LLP, the Applicants’ Counsel.
Iain Dixon provided opening remarks and reviewed relevant legislation as set out in section 275
of the Vancouver Charter and section(s) 28.1(3) of Licence By-law No. 4450.
In support of the allegations set out in the evidence brief, Iain Dixon called Alina Urloiu, Deputy
Chief Licence Inspector, as a witness for the City of Vancouver.
Iain Dixon along with Panel members directed questions to the witness.
The Applicant’s Counsel also directed questions to the witness.
The Applicant’s Counsel called Michael Enriquez, the Applicant as a witness.
The Applicant’s Counsel along with Panel members directed questions to the witness.
The Applicant responded to questions.
Iain Dixon, provided closing submissions based on the evidence and reviewed the reasons for
the business licence referral to Council.
The Applicants Counsel provided closing submissions.
PANEL MEMBERS DISCUSSION
MOVED by Councillor Meiszner
SECONDED by Councillor Bligh

Special Council Meeting (Business Licence Hearing)
Minutes, Wednesday, July 16, 2025 3
THAT the Business License Hearing Panel deny the application made by Michael
Enriquez (the “Applicant”) for a 2025 business licence (“Business Licence”) for a short-
term rental accommodation at 2606-1068 Hornby Street, on the basis that: the Premises
does not appear to be the Applicant’s principal residence based on the evidence
presented.
CARRIED UNAIMOUSLY (Vote No. 10867)
