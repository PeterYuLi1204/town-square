SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
March 19, 2020
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on
Thursday, March 19, 2020, at, 10:26 am in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the
purpose of declaring a state of local emergency, resolving to grant additional authority under the
Emergency Program Act and amending an adopted motion to re-purpose community grants to
better support the community’s COVID-19 response. This Special Council meeting was
convened by electronic means as authorized by Part 14 of the Procedure By-law and the
Vancouver Charter.

2. Amendments to Community Services Grants to Allow Community Response to
COVID-19
March 18, 2020
Sandra Singh, General Manager, Arts, Culture and Community Services, responded to
questions by way of teleconference.
MOVED by Councillor De Genova
SECONDED by Councillor Hardwick
A. THAT Council approve the following amendments to Council’s prior approval of
2020 Community Services and Other Social Grants (RTS No. 13579) and 2020
Renter Services Grants and Renter Service Update (RTS No. 13412) (the “Prior
Resolution”):
i. With respect to all Social Services Agencies (as defined below), the
General Manager of Arts, Culture and Community Services (or their
designate) (“GM of ACCS”) be authorized to accelerate the disbursement of
the 2020 Social Grants (as defined below) to allow the community to
respond to COVID-19 as quickly as necessary.
ii. With respect to all Social Services Agencies, the conditions of their
respective grant agreements are now amended to allow the use of the grant
for the following additional purposes subject to the following conditions:

Special Council Meeting
Minutes, Thursday, March 19, 2020 5
• The grant must still be used for the benefit of the same members of
the community that the original grant conditions required (the “Target
Community”).
• The applicable Social Service Agency may utilize the grant for any
purpose that supports the Target Community in mitigating or
preventing the transmission of COVID-19 or assisting any member of
the Target Community who contracts COVID-19 (the “Re-Purpose”).
• The Re-Purpose will be deemed to include any such costs as are
reasonably necessary to protect the health of the employees and
volunteers of the Social Service Agency in carrying out the
Re-Purpose.
• All of the audit and records-keeping requirements of the grant
agreement will continue to apply to all expenditures made in
connection with the Re-Purpose.
B. THAT, if Council approves A above, the GM of ACCS (or their designate) is
authorized to issue all such amendments to the existing grant agreements for the
2020 Social Grants and issue all such other communications and execute any
and all such documents as considered by the GM of ACCS reasonably
necessary to implement the intent and effect of A above including all such
clarifications and additional terms and conditions as considered by the GM of
ACCS reasonably necessary in the circumstances.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY AND A
BY THE REQUIRED MAJORITY (Vote No. 05713)
