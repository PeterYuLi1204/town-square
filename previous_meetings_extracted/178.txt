WELCOME
The Mayor acknowledged we are on the unceded homelands of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh People. We thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to working
with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Mayor also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s team
members who work hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and
play.

generally as presented in Appendix A of the Referral Report dated June 11, 2024,
entitled “Modernizing Home-Based Business Regulations”;
WHEREAS at the time of the enactment of the Zoning and Development By-law
amendments, the General Manager of Planning, Urban Design and Sustainability was
instructed to bring forward for approval by Council amendments to the documents as
identified in Table 1 below and generally as set out in Appendix B of the Referral Report
dated June 11, 2024, entitled “Modernizing Home-Based Business Regulations”;
AND WHEREAS the Zoning and Development By-law Amendments were enacted by
Council on October 8, 2024, and have come into force and effect.
THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED THAT the below amendments to the Artist Studio
Guidelines, Arts and Culture Studio Technical Guidelines, and Live-Work Guidelines are
hereby adopted, and are to come into effect on October 22, 2024.
