SPECIAL COUNCIL
COURT OF REVISION – LOCAL IMPROVEMENTS
MEETING MINUTES
JUNE 22, 2017
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on
Thursday, June 22, 2017, at 6:10 pm, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall,
for the purpose of holding a Court of Revision pertaining to local improvement projects.
PRESENT: Councillor Raymond Louie, Acting Mayor
Councillor George Affleck
Councillor Adriane Carr
Councillor Heather Deal
Councillor Kerry Jang
Councillor Geoff Meggs
ABSENT: Mayor Gregor Robertson
Councillor Elizabeth Ball (Medical Leave)
Councillor Melissa De Genova (Leave of Absence)
Councillor Andrea Reimer (Civic Business)
Councillor Tim Stevenson (Leave of Absence)
CITY CLERK’S OFFICE: Nicole Ludwig, Meeting Coordinator
RESOLVE INTO COURT OF REVISION
MOVED by Councillor Jang
SECONDED by Councillor Deal
THAT this Council resolve itself into Court of Revision for the purpose of hearing
complaints against the proposed assessments or the accuracy of frontage
measurements, or any other complaint which is by law cognizable by the
Court, Acting Mayor Louie in the Chair.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY
1. Projects by Petition
The Court had before it a report dated June 5, 2017, from the Collector of Taxes to the Local
Improvement Court of Revision No. 637 concerning Projects by Petition, and included the
following:
Item No. Project Type Designated Area
637/001 Lane Paving Lane east of Granville Street from the lane south of Nanton
Avenue to Devonshire Crescent and the lane south of Nanton
Avenue from the lane east of Granville Street to Cartier Street.
637/002 Lane Paving Lane south of Napier Street from Cassiar Street to Skeena Street.

Item No. Project Type Designated Area
637/003 Lane Paving Lane south of 12th Avenue from Camosun Street to Crown Street.
637/004 Lane Paving Lane south of 17th Avenue from Quebec Street to the lane west of
Main Street.
637/005 Lane Lighting Lane east of Todd Street from Vanness Avenue to Euclid Avenue.
The Collector of Taxes certified that the projects in the report dated June 5, 2017, entitled
“Report of the Collector of Taxes to the Local Improvement Court of Revision No. 637 –
Projects by Petition, have been requested by the majority of the assessed owners by means of
a sufficiently signed petition.
a. Consideration of Errors in the Special Assessment Roll
The Acting Mayor called for speakers with respect to any errors of the Special
Assessment Roll and none were present.
Dane Doleman, Engineering Services, drew the Court’s attention Appendix A of the
Collector of Taxes report noted above, which corrected a discrepancy with the
assessed frontage, which resulted in an increased total estimated cost for project
637/001.
b. Motion to Certify the Correctness of the Special Assessment Roll
MOVED by Councillor Deal
SECONDED by Councillor Jang
THAT the report of the Special Assessment Roll for Court No. 637 as summarized in the
Report dated June 5, 2017, entitled “Report of the Collector of Taxes to the Local
Improvement Court of Revision No. 637 – Projects by Petition”, be approved.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY (Vote No. 02047)
RISE FROM COURT OF REVISION
MOVED by Councillor Meggs
SECONDED by Councillor Jang
THAT the Court of Revision rise and report.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY
2. Adopt the Report of the Court of Revision - June 22, 2017
MOVED by Councillor Jang
SECONDED by Councillor Deal
THAT Council adopt the report of the Court of Revision – Local Improvements No. 637
dated June 22, 2017.
