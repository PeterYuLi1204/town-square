3. CD-1 Rezoning: 418-496 Alexander Street
An application by TL Housing Solutions Ltd. was considered as follows:
Summary: To rezone 418-496 Alexander Street from DEOD (Downtown Eastside/
Oppenheimer) District to CD-1 (Comprehensive Development) District, to
permit the development of a 16-storey mixed-use building containing 181
social housing units, a social enterprise space and childcare facility. A building
height of 48.8 m (160 ft.) and a floor space ratio (FSR) of 5.67 are proposed.
Council also had before it a memorandum from the General Manager of Planning, Urban Design
and Sustainability, dated July 4, 2022, entitled “CD-1 Rezoning: 418-496 Alexander Street –
Amendment to the Engineering Condition”, which confirmed that a sewer upgrade is not
required. This finding was following the completion of a flow monitoring investigation in the
area.
