1. Framework Process for Resolution of Leasehold Strata End-of-Lease Issues
June 29, 2017
The General Manager, Real Estate and Facilities Management, along with the Manager,
Property Endowment Fund, Real Estate Services, provided a report back on a framework
process for negotiating a resolution to issues arising from the approaching end of leases in
False Creek South.
Richard Evans, Chair, *RePlan, spoke in support of the report and acknowledged staff for their
collaborative efforts throughout the process.
2. Theatre Rental Grant Allocations - Fall 2017 and Spring 2018
June 27, 2017
At the Regular Council meeting on July 11, 2017, Vancouver City Council referred this matter
to the Standing Committee on City Finance and Services meeting on July 12, 2017, to hear
from speakers.
The Committee heard from one speaker who sought clarification on why his organization was
ineligible for a grant, provided background on the organization’s participation in local arts
and culture, and sought Council’s reconsideration of their application.
The Director, Vancouver Civic Theatres, along with the Managing Director, Cultural Services,
and staff from Vancouver Civic Theatres, responded to questions.

Standing Committee of Council on City Finance and Services
Minutes, Wednesday, July 12, 2017 2
MOVED by Councillor Louie
THAT the Committee recommend to Council
A. THAT Council approve Theatre Rental Grants totalling $1,101,669 to
37 organizations, as listed in Appendix A of the Administrative Report dated
June 27, 2017, entitled “Theatre Rental Grant Allocations - Fall 2017 and
Spring 2018”, for use of Vancouver Civic Theatres (VCT) from September to
December 31, 2017; the source of funds to be the remaining balance in the
2017 Theatre Rental Grant budget and forecast net surplus projected for
VCT’s 2017 Operating Budget.
B. THAT Council approve, in advance of the 2018 Operating Budget, Theatre Rental
Grants totalling $1,790,389 for VCT uses from January 1 - August 31, 2018, to
40 organizations, as listed in Appendix B of the Administrative Report dated
June 27, 2017, entitled “Theatre Rental Grant Allocations - Fall 2017 and Spring
2018”; the source of funds to be the 2018 Theatre Rental Grants budget, subject
to Council’s approval of the City’s 2018 Operating Budget.
C. THAT Council approve the re-allocation of funds from any underused or
cancelled Theatre Rental Grants on an on-going annual basis, as approved by
the General Manager of Community Services to a maximum of $200,000 per
annum.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY AND
A AND B BY THE REQUIRED MAJORITY (Vote No. 02112)
The Committee adjourned at 10:29 am.
