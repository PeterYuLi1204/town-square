SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
DECEMBER 11, 2018
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Tuesday,
December 11, 2018, at 9:37 am, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purposes
of receiving a presentation from staff and hearing from speakers on the 2019 Capital and
Operating Budget.

WELCOME
The Mayor acknowledged we are on the unceded territories of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh Nations and we thank them for having cared for this land since time immemorial.
At 9:41 am, Councillor De Genova declared conflict of interest under Section 145.2(6)(a) of the
Vancouver Charter on agenda item 1.f. 2019 Capital and Operating Budget, on a portion of

Special Council Meeting
Minutes, Tuesday, December 11, 2018 2
1. 2019 Capital and Operating Budget
Patrice Impey, General Manager, Finance, Risk and Business Planning, provided a presentation
on the 2019 Capital and Operating Budget.
Council heard from twenty-four speakers; thirteen who spoke in general support of the
2019 Capital and Operating Budget. Seven speakers noted concerns and offered comments
regarding the budget for the Vancouver Police Department. Two speakers spoke in opposition
and two were in the other category.
Sadhu Johnston, City Manager, along with staff from Finance, Risk and Business Planning,
Arts, Culture and Community Services, Civic Engagement and Communications, Engineering
Services, and Fire and Rescue Services, responded to questions.
