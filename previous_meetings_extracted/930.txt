SPECIAL COUNCIL
COURT OF REVISION – LOCAL IMPROVEMENTS

JUNE 11, 2019

June 11, 2019, at 5:20 pm, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for
the purpose of holding a Court of Revision pertaining to local improvement projects.
