SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
May 7, 2025
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Wednesday, May 7,
2025, at 1:02 pm, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of considering
a By-law to amend Zoning and Development By-law No. 3575 to rezone an area to CD-1 and
consequential amendments to by-laws.

Bonnie Kennett, Meeting Coordinator
WELCOME
The Mayor acknowledged we are on the unceded homelands of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh People. We thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to working
with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
