electronic means as authorized in Part 14 of the Procedure By-law.

WELCOME
The Chair acknowledged we are on the unceded homelands of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh People. We thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to working
with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Chair also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s staff who work
hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and play.
The Chair on behalf of the Committee provided a welcome to the newly appointed External
Advisory Liaison Member Sophia Tsui. The Auditor General provided a brief introduction noting
professional achievements.

CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY
1. The Permitting Program Cost Recovery Model Audit Report Transmittal
The Auditor General and Audit Principal provided an overview of the item, as well as a
presentation. The Auditor General and his staff, along with Patrice Impey, General Manager of
Finance, Risk and Supply Chain Management, Andrea Law, General Manager of Development,
Buildings and Licensing, and Theresa O’Donnell, Director of Planning and General Manager,
Planning, Urban Design and Sustainability, responded to questions. All staff spoke in support of
the recommendations as identified in the report.
MOVED by Councillor Zhou
SECONDED by Councillor Kirby-Yung
THAT the Committee recommend to Council
THAT the Auditor General Committee receive the Auditor General’s Report “The
Permitting Program Cost Recovery Model”, dated May 2023;
FURTHER THAT the above noted report’s eight recommendations be endorsed.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY (Vote No. 09306)
