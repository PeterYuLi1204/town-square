WELCOME
The Chair acknowledged we are on the unceded territories of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh Nations and we thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to
working with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Chair also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s staff who work
hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and play.

Council COVID-19 Recovery Committee
Minutes, July 3, 2020 3
2. Process for Completing CCRC End of July Report and Recommendations
MOVED by Councillor Carr
THAT Council delegate the drafting of the Council COVID Recovery Committee (CCRC)
final report to a Working Group comprised of the Ad-hoc Ranking Sub-committee
(Councillors Boyle, Fry and Kirby-Yung) and CCRC Liaisons (Councillors Carr and
Dominato) with the following instructions:
A. THAT the Working Group provide an update of the draft report at the next CCRC
meeting.
B. THAT the Working Group prepare the report for consideration by the CCRC at its
meeting of July 29, 2020.
C. THAT, as per the CCRC Terms of Reference, the report comprise the findings
and recommendations of the CCRC in order that they be evaluated by staff and,
as appropriate, integrated into the City’s COVID-19 Economic and Community
Recovery Program and/or other staff workstreams underway, and brought
forward to City Council with analysis and recommendations.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY (Vote No. 06159)
(Councillors De Genova and Hardwick absent for the vote)
