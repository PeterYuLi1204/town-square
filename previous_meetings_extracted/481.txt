SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
(BUSINESS LICENCE HEARING)
JUNE 15, 2022
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Wednesday,
June 15, 2022, at 6:05 pm, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of
convening a Business Licence Hearing to determine whether a 2022 Business Licence should
be issued to Adam Rentz (the “Applicant”), for a short-term rental accommodation located at
3429 West 32nd Avenue (the “Premises”). This Special Council meeting was convened by
electronic means as authorized by Part 14 of the Procedure By-law and the Vancouver Charter.
PRESENT: Councillor Rebecca Bligh, Chair
Councillor Sarah Kirby-Yung

Robert LeBlanc, City Prosecutor, Legal Services, was present on behalf of the City of
Vancouver. Adam Rentz, the Applicant, was present on their own behalf.
Mr. LeBlanc provided opening remarks and reviewed relevant legislation as set out in section
275 of the Vancouver Charter and sections 4(4) and 25.1(3) of Licence By-law No. 4450.

Special Council Meeting (Business Licence Hearing)
Minutes, Wednesday, June 15, 2022 3
In support of the allegations set out in the evidence brief, Mr. LeBlanc called Koji Miyaji,
Assistant Director, Community Standards, Development, Buildings and Licencing, as a witness.
Mr. LeBlanc directed questions to Mr. Miyaji.
The Applicant provided opening remarks in defence of the allegations submitted by the witness.
The Applicant responded to questions from the Panel and Mr. LeBlanc.
Mr. LeBlanc provided closing submissions based on the evidence.
PANEL MEMBERS DISCUSSION
MOVED by Councillor Kirby-Yung
SECONDED by Councillor Wiebe
THAT the Business Licence Hearing Panel refuse to issue a 2022 Business Licence to
Adam Rentz (the “Applicant”), for a short-term rental accommodation located at
3429 West 32nd Avenue, Vancouver, BC (the “Premises), on the basis that:
• the Applicant has failed to satisfy requirements related to proof of residence or
provided sufficient or appropriate documentation to credibly prove the above-
noted address is their primary residence.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY (Vote No. 08512)
ADJOURNMENT
MOVED by Councillor Wiebe
SECONDED by Councillor Kirby-Yung
THAT the meeting be adjourned.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY
The Business Licence Hearing Panel adjourned at 7:15 pm.
