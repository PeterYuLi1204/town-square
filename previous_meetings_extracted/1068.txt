BUSINESS LICENCE HEARING MINUTES
MARCH 15, 2018

Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, to consider the recommendation by the Deputy Chief Licence

business premises located at 2234 Kingsway, Vancouver, British Columbia (the “Premises”).

Iain Dixon, Assistant Director – Regulatory Litigation, Legal Department, was present on behalf
of the City of Vancouver. Sandy Ross of Johnson Doyle Sugarman and Ferguson, was present
on behalf of Van De Nguyen (the Licencee).
In opening, Mr. Ross noted the absence of a Vietnamese interpreter for Mr. Nguyen. Mr. Dixon
then submitted that it would be inappropriate to proceed with the Business Licence Hearing in
the absence of an interpreter, and advised that the hearing be adjourned to the next available
Business Licence Hearing date.
