SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING
COURT OF REVISION – BUSINESS IMPROVEMENT AREAS
MINUTES
FEBRUARY 8, 2024
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Thursday,
February 8, 2024, at 3:00 pm, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of
hearing from assessed property owners regarding errors in the names of the owners of the
property and the measurements of the property, including the frontage or any other property
measurement, and any variation applied. This Special Council meeting was convened by
electronic means as authorized in Part 14 of the Procedure By-law.

WELCOME
The Chair acknowledged we are on the unceded homelands of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh People and we thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to
working with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
1. DELEGATIONS CONCERNING ERRORS
The Court of Revision had before it special assessment notification letters that were sent on or
before Tuesday, November 29, 2023, to the assessed property owners within the following
Business Improvement Areas (BIAs):
a) Fraser Street Business Improvement Area (BIA) Renewal
b) Gastown Business Improvement Area (BIA) Renewal
c) Point Grey Village Business Improvement Area (BIA) Renewal
d) South Granville Business Improvement Area (BIA) Renewal
e) Strathcona Business Improvement Area (BIA) Renewal

f) Vancouver Chinatown Business Improvement Area (BIA) Renewal
g) Victoria Drive Business Improvement Area (BIA) Renewal
h) Yaletown Business Improvement Area (BIA) Renewal
i) Mt. Pleasant Business Improvement Area (BIA) South Main Expansion
Staff from the BIA Program advised that no correspondence has been received with respect to
errors in the name of assessed owners or the measurements of the property, including the
frontage or any other property measurement and any variation applied pursuant to sections
508 (g) and (j) of the Vancouver Charter.
This represents all correspondence received up to the deadline of 5 pm on Friday,
January 26, 2024, as specified in the notification letters.
The Chair called for speakers relating only to the above-noted errors and none were present.
