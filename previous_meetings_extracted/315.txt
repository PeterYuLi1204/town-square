WELCOME
The Chair acknowledged we are on the unceded homelands of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh People. We thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to working
with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Chair also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s staff who work
hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and play.

Auditor General Committee
Minutes, Thursday, October 05, 2023 2
1. Update on Whistleblower Policy
The Auditor General provided a verbal progress update, which was in consultation with Andrew
Naklicki, Chief Human Resources Officer, as well as responded to questions.
This item was for information only, and therefore did not require a vote.
2. Office of the Auditor General 2024 Budget Request
The Auditor General provided a presentation of the item, as well as responded to questions.
MOVED by Councillor Kirby-Yung
SECONDED by Councillor Zhou
THAT the Committee recommend to Council
THAT the Auditor General Committee endorse the Auditor General’s 2024 budget
request of $2,412,000 to fund the operations of the Office of the Auditor General for
2024; and
FURTHER THAT the endorsement be recommended to Council for approval.
