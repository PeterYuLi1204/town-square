COUNCIL MEMBERS’ MOTIONS
1. FCM Resolution: Enhancing Accessibility Under the Federal Plastic Straw Ban
MOVED by Councillor Dominato
SECONDED by Councillor Zhou
THAT the Committee recommend to Council
THAT Council endorse the following resolution to be forwarded to the Federation of
Canadian Municipalities (FCM) Board:
RESOLUTION
MOTION: Enhancing Accessibility Under the Federal Plastic Straw Ban
WHEREAS the federal Single-Use Plastics Prohibition Regulations limit the sale
and distribution of certain single use plastics, including plastic straws;
WHEREAS the federal Single-Use Plastics Prohibition Regulations have very
narrow exceptions for the sale and distribution of plastic straws, and does not
sufficiently accommodate the special needs of persons with disabilities and
medical issues;

Standing Committee of Council on Policy and Strategic Priorities
Minutes, Wednesday, September 25, 2024 6
AND WHEREAS persons with disabilities and medical issues may require an
“accessible straw” for use at home, the workplace or when eating out.
THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED THAT FCM urge the federal government to
enhance accessibility under the Single-Use Plastics Prohibition Regulations by
allowing the following:
i. Food vendors to provide an “accessible straw” (flexible plastic straw,
individually wrapped in paper) when requested;
ii. Retailers to display packages of flexible plastic straws in stores; and
iii. Flexible plastic straws attached to beverage containers like drink
boxes.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY (Vote No. 10221)
The Committee adjourned at 10:07 am.
