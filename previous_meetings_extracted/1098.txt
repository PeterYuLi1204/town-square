SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
DECEMBER 6, 2017
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Wednesday,
December 6, 2017, at 9:42 am, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose
of receiving a presentation from staff and hearing from speakers on the 2018 Capital and
Operating Budget.

WELCOME
The Acting Mayor acknowledged we are on the unceded traditional territory of the Musqueam,
Squamish and Tsleil-Waututh First Nations.
1. 2018 Capital and Operating Budget
Patrice Impey, General Manager, Finance, Risk & Business Planning, provided a presentation
on the 2018 Capital and Operating Budget.
* * * * *
At 9:53 am, during the presentation, the Mayor assumed the Chair
for the remainder of the meeting.
* * * * *

Special Council Meeting
Minutes, Wednesday, December 6, 2017 2
Council heard from nine speakers; eight who spoke in support of the 2018 Capital and
Operating Budget. Some of the speakers noted concerns regarding staffing levels and
benefits, one speaker requested additional funding and others praised the efforts of the City
to increase public safety. One speaker was opposed and offered comments regarding the
budget for the Vancouver Police Department.
* * * * *
At noon, it was
MOVED by Councillor Deal
SECONDED by Councillor Jang
THAT the meeting be extended to complete the business on the agenda.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY AND
BY THE REQUIRED MAJORITY
(Councillors Reimer and Stevenson absent for the vote)

At 3:12 pm, the Mayor acknowledged December 6 as the National Day of Remembrance
and Action on Violence Against Women. A moment of silence was taken to remember and
honour the fourteen women killed in 1989 at l’École Polytechnic de Montréal and
recommit to tenacious action to end all forms of violence against women and girls.
