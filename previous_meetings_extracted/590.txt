WELCOME
The Mayor acknowledged we are on the unceded territories of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh Nations and we thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to
working with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Mayor also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s staff who work
hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and play.
CONDOLENCES – Former Mayor Philip Owen
The Mayor expressed his sincere condolences to the family and friends of former Vancouver
Mayor Philip Owen on behalf of Vancouver City Council, the City of Vancouver, and the entire
community.
PUBLIC NOTICE – Pursuant to Section 164.1(2) of the Vancouver Charter, the Mayor gave
public notice that Council will give consideration to amend Procedure By-law No. 12577 at the
Council meeting on October 19, 2021.
