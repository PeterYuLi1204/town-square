SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
NOVEMBER 12, 13 ,18 AND 19, 2025
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Wednesday,
November 12, 2025, at 9:33 am, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose
of hearing presentations and speakers to the draft 2026 Budget. Due to time constraints, the
meeting recessed and reconvened on Thursday, November 13, 2025, at 1:06 pm, Tuesday,
November 18, 2025, at 3:02 pm, and Wednesday, November 19, 2025, at 3 pm. This Special
Council meeting was convened in person and via electronic means as authorized by Part 14 of
the Procedure By-law.

Special Council Meeting
Minutes, November 12, 13, 18 and 19, 2025 2
WELCOME
The Mayor acknowledged we are on the unceded homelands of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh People. We thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to working
with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Mayor also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s team
members who work hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and
play.
1. 2026 Capital and Operating Budget
a) 2026 Annual Review of Water Rates and Water Works By-law Amendments
b) 2026 Annual Review of Sewer Rates – Sewer and Watercourse By-law
c) 2026 Solid Waste Utility Fees and By-law Changes
d) 2026 False Creek Neighbourhood Energy Utility Customer Rates
e) 2026 Engineering Fees
f) 2026 Fee Increases for Rezoning, Development, Building and Other Related
Applications and Permits
g) Proposed 2026 Operating and Capital Budget
The General Manager of Finance and Supply Chain Management, along with staff from the
City’s Leadership Team, provided a presentation on the Proposed 2026 Operating and Capital
Budget.

MOVED by Councillor Klassen
SECONDED by Councillor Zhou
THAT under section 2.8(a) of the Procedure By-law, Council extend the meeting past

carried
AMENDMENT MOVED by Councillor Fry
SECONDED by Councillor Bligh
THAT the following be added to the end of the motion:
FURTHER THAT, under section 5.5 of the Procedure By-law, Council suspend

REQUIRED MAJORITY with Councillor Montague absent for vote.
FINAL MOTION AS APPROVED
THAT under section 2.8(a) of the Procedure By-law, Council extend the meeting past
noon in order to finish the second round of questions.
