SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
(BUSINESS LICENCE HEARING)
MARCH 5, 2024
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Tuesday, March 5, 2024,
at 9:35 am, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of holding a Business
Licence Hearing to consider the decision of the Chief Licence Inspector to suspend the 2023
business licence (“Business Licence”) issued to 1394867 BC Ltd. (the “Licencee”) for business
premises located at 247 W Broadway, Vancouver, British Columbia (the “Premises”). This
Special Council meeting was convened by electronic means as authorized by Part 14 of the
Procedure By-law and the Vancouver Charter.
PRESENT: Councillor Pete Fry, Chair
Councillor Adriane Carr
Councillor Mike Klassen
CITY CLERK’S OFFICE: Cassia Nasralla, Meeting Coordinator
Olivia Kam, Meeting Coordinator

Robert LeBlanc, Litigation Counsel, Legal Services, was present on behalf of the City of
Vancouver. Jack Lloyd, Barrister and Solicitor, was also present to represent 1394867 BC Ltd,
the Licencee.
Mr. LeBlanc provided opening remarks and reviewed relevant legislation as set out in section
277 of the Vancouver Charter and sections 4(12) of the City of Vancouver’s Licence By-law No.
4450.
In support of the allegations set out in the above-noted evidence, Mr. LeBlanc called Sarah
Hicks, Chief Licence Inspector, as a witness for the City of Vancouver.
Mr. LeBlanc, along with Panel members, directed questions to the City of Vancouver’s witness.
Mr. Lloyd also posed questions to the witness.
Mr. Lloyd provided opening remarks and responded to the allegations submitted by the witness.
Mr. Lloyd called Dana Larsen as witness for the Licencee. Mr. Lloyd directed questions to the
Licencee’s witness.
Mr. Lloyd, along with the Licencee’s witness, responded to questions from the Panel and Mr.
LeBlanc.
Mr. LeBlanc provided closing submissions based on the evidence and reviewed the reasons for

PANEL MEMBERS DISCUSSION
MOVED by Councillor Klassen
THAT the Business Licence Hearing Panel suspend the 2023 Business Licence issued
to 1394867 BC Ltd. (the “Licencee”) for business premises located at 247 W Broadway,
Vancouver, on the basis of section 277 of the Vancouver Charter and sections 4(12) of
the City of Vancouver’s Licence By-law No. 4450.
not put
