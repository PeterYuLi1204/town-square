REPORT REFERENCE
1. A City-wide Plan for Vancouver: Report Back on General Planning and
Engagement Process
July 2, 2019
Gil Kelley, General Manager, Planning, Urban Design and Sustainability, provided a
presentation on a City-wide Plan for Vancouver that is a long-range vision and plan to guide
future change and growth, a key priority of Council. The plan is designed to be flexible and
adaptive and is informed by equity, accessibility, spatial justice and the right to housing and
includes a robust and collaborative engagement process with the public.
