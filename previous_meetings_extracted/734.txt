SPECIAL COUNCIL
COURT OF REVISION – LOCAL IMPROVEMENTS
MEETING MINUTES
NOVEMBER 3, 2020
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Tuesday,
November 3, 2020, at 4:07 pm, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for
the purpose of holding a Court of Revision pertaining to local improvement projects. This
Special Council meeting was convened by electronic means as authorized under Part 14 of the
Procedure By-law and the Vancouver Charter and the Order of the Minister of Public Safety and
Solicitor General of the Province of British Columbia – Emergency Program Act, Ministerial
Order No. M192.
PRESENT: Councillor Lisa Dominato, Deputy Mayor

RESOLVE INTO COURT OF REVISION
MOVED by Councillor De Genova
SECONDED by Councillor Kirby-Yung
THAT this Council resolve itself into Court of Revision for the purpose of hearing
complaints against the proposed assessments or the accuracy of frontage
measurements, or any other complaint which is by law cognizable by the Court, Deputy
Mayor Dominato in the Chair.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY

1. Projects by Petition
The Court had before it a Report dated November 3, 2020, entitled “Report of the Collector of
Taxes to the Local Improvement Court of Revision No. 641 – Projects by Petition” from the
Collector of Taxes to the Local Improvement Court of Revision No. 641 concerning Projects by
Petition as follows:
Item No. Project Type Designated Area
641/001 Lane Paving Lane south of West 36th Avenue from Highbury Street to Alma
Street
641/002 Lane Paving Lane south of West 11th Avenue from Sasamat Street to Trimble
Street
641/003 Lane Paving Lane east of Haig Street from West 64th Avenue to West 67th
Avenue
641/004 Lane Paving Lane east of Angus Drive from West 52th Avenue to West 54th
Avenue
641/005 Lane Paving Lanes south of Parker Street from Kelowna Street to Nootka
Street
641/006 Lane Speed Lane east of St. Catherines Street from East 35th Avenue to East
Humps 37th Avenue
641/007 Lane Lighting Lane east of Kaslo Street from East 27th Avenue to East 29th
Avenue
The Collector of Taxes certified that the projects in the above-noted report have been requested
by the majority of the assessed owners by means of a sufficiently signed petition.
(a) Consideration of Errors in the Special Assessment Roll
The Chair called for speakers with respect to any errors on the Special Assessment Roll and
none were present.
Engineering Services staff confirmed there were no errors on the Special Assessment Roll.
(b) Motion to Certify the Correctness of the Special Assessment Roll
MOVED by Councillor De Genova
SECONDED by Councillor Carr
THAT the report of the Special Assessment Roll for Court No. 641 as summarized in the
Report dated November 3, 2020, entitled “Report of the Collector of Taxes to the Local
Improvement Court of Revision No. 641 – Projects by Petition”, be approved.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY

3. Delegations Concerning the Projects by Petition
Deputy Mayor Dominato called for speakers regarding the applications for Projects by Petition
noted above. Council heard from the following:
• Project 641-001: 2 speakers in support
• Project 641-002: 2 speakers in support and 2 speakers opposed
• Project 641-004: 2 speakers opposed
• Project 641-005: 1 speaker opposed
• Project 641-007: 1 speaker opposed
Following the hearing of speakers, Engineering staff responded to questions.
Council agreed to separate the vote on the components of the motion.
