SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
MAY 20, 2021
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Thursday, May 20, 2021,
at 6:01 pm in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of convening a
meeting which was closed to the public. This Special Council meeting was convened by
electronic means as authorized under sections 2.5 and Part 14 of the Procedure By-law and the
Vancouver Charter and under the Order of the Minister of Public Safety and Solicitor General of
the Province of British Columbia – Emergency Program Act, updated Ministerial Order No.
M192.

WELCOME
The Mayor acknowledged we are on the unceded territories of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh Nations and we thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to
working with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Mayor also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s staff who work
hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and play.
