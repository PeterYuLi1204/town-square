SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
May 14, 2024
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Tuesday, May 14, 2024,
at 3 pm, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of receiving the
Provincial Housing Targets Order – 6 Month Interim Report. This Special Council meeting was
convened in person and via electronic means as authorized by Part 14 of the Procedure By-law.

WELCOME
The Mayor acknowledged we are on the unceded homelands of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh People. We thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to working
with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Mayor also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s team
members who work hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and
play.
1. Provincial Housing Targets Order – 6 Month Interim Report:
October 1, 2023 – March 31, 2024
May 10, 2024
Staff from Planning, Urban Design and Sustainability provided a presentation and responded to
questions.

Special Council Meeting
Minutes, Tuesday, May 14, 2024 2
* * * * *
Prior to questions of staff, it was
MOVED by Councillor Kirby-Yung
SECONDED by Councillor Zhou
THAT under section 5.4(d) of the Procedure By-law, Council be permitted to ask a
second round of questions to staff.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY
* * * * *
Following questions to staff, Council heard from one speaker who spoke in opposition to the
recommendations, and two speakers who spoke to other aspects of the recommendations.
MOVED by Councillor Kirby-Yung
SECONDED by Councillor Zhou
A. THAT Council receive for information the Housing Targets Interim Report:
October 1, 2023 – March 31, 2024, as contained in Appendix A of the Report
dated May 10, 2024, entitled “Provincial Housing Target Order – 6 Month Interim
Report: October 1, 2023 – March 31, 2024”.
B. THAT Council direct Staff to submit the Housing Targets Interim Report:
October 1, 2023 – March 31, 2024, as contained in Appendix A of the Report
dated May 10, 2024, entitled “Provincial Housing Target Order – 6 Month Interim
Report: October 1, 2023 – March 31, 2024”, to the Province by May 15, 2024, in
order to comply with the requirements under the Provincial Housing Supply Act.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY (Vote No. 09984)
ADJOURNMENT
MOVED by Councillor Zhou
SECONDED by Councillor Fry
THAT the meeting be adjourned.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY
The Special Council adjourned at 4:38 pm.
