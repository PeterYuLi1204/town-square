A Meeting of the Mayor’s Budget Task Force Implementation Oversight Committee was held on
Tuesday, June 18, 2024, at 1:00 PM, in the Mayor’s Ceremonial Boardroom, Third Floor, City
Hall.

ALSO PRESENT: Mellisa Morphy, Mayor’s Deputy Chief of Staff
REPORTS
1. Finance Committee Terms of Reference
The City Manager, along with the City Clerk, responded to comments and questions on draft
Terms of Reference for a proposed Finance Committee.
The Committee agreed, by consensus, that staff will present recommendations for Finance
Committee structure at the July 2, 2024 Committee meeting.
2. Finance Committee Implementation Timing
The City Manager, along with the City Clerk, responded to comments and questions on the
timing of the proposed Finance Committee implementation.
3. Standing Item – Work in Progress / Prioritization of Recommendations
The Committee agreed, by consensus, to review recommendations of the Task Force and bring
forward questions for staff at the next Committee meeting.
