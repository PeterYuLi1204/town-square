SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
March 10, 2023
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Friday, March 10, 2023,
at 9:30 am, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for Council to enact the Development
Potential Tax Relief Declaration By-law, 2023. This Special Council meeting was convened by
electronic means as authorized in Part 14 of the Procedure By-law.

Cassia Nasralla, Meeting Coordinator
WELCOME
The Acting Mayor acknowledged we are on the unceded homelands of the Musqueam,
Squamish, and Tsleil-Waututh People. We thank them for having cared for this land and look
forward to working with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Acting Mayor also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s team
members who work hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and
play.
1. Development Potential Tax Relief Declaration By-law, 2023
MOVED by Councillor Dominato
SECONDED by Councillor Kirby-Yung
THAT Council enact the by-law listed on the agenda for this meeting as number 1, and
authorize the Mayor and City Clerk to sign and seal the enacted by-law.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY
