REPORT TO COUNCIL
COUNCIL COVID-19 RECOVERY COMMITTEE
JULY 15, 2020
A meeting of the Council COVID-19 Recovery Committee on was held on Wednesday,
July 15, 2020, at 9:31 am in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall. This Council meeting
was convened by electronic means as authorized under the Order of the Minister of Public
Safety and Solicitor General of the Province of British Columbia - the Emergency Program Act,
Ministerial Order No. M139.

WELCOME
The Chair acknowledged we are on the unceded territories of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh Nations and we thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to
working with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Chair also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s staff who work
hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and play.
COMMITTEE OF THE WHOLE
MOVED by Councillor Carr
SECONDED by Councillor Bligh
THAT this Council resolve itself into Committee of the Whole, Councillor Hardwick in the
Chair, to consider the agenda items.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY

2. Potential Recommendations Arising from Presentations
Councillor Carr summarized a list of potential recommendations from guest speakers for the
Committee’s consideration. Councillors Swanson, Kirby-Yung, Bligh and Dominato provided
additional suggestions to Councillor Carr who then encouraged the Committee to submit any
additional suggestions for recommendations.
3. Update on Draft CCRC Final Report
Councillor Carr provided an outline of the process the Committee has undertaken and an
overview of the report’s outline and reminded the Committee of the report’s deadline of
July 29, 2020. Councillor Carr advised a draft copy of the report would be shared with Council in
order for them to provide feedback in advance of the final version of the report.
