SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
MARCH 9, 2019
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Saturday, March 9, 2019,
at 9:06 am, in the Joyce Walley Room, Museum of Vancouver, 1100 Chestnut Street,
Vancouver, BC, for the purpose of engaging Council in a workshop to discuss the scoping of the
City-wide Plan.

Special Council Meeting
Minutes, Saturday, March 9, 2019 2
1. City-wide Plan Scoping Workshop
Gil Kelley, General Manager of Planning, Urban Design and Sustainability, provided an
overview of the workshop objectives and introduced Daniel Iacofano, Facilitator.
Mr. Iacofano asked Council a series of questions related to the process and scope of the
City-wide Plan. Council provided input on their aspirations for the City-wide Plan process, the
major opportunities and challenges they see with regard to the City-wide Plan and what a
successful City-wide Plan would look like to them.
* * * * *
MOVED by Councillor De Genova
SECONDED by Councillor Boyle
THAT Council recess for 15 minutes.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY
* * * * *
Council recessed at 10:57 am and reconvened at 11:13 am.
* * * * *
Following the recess, Mr. Iacofano continued to engage Council with additional questions
related to the City-wide Plan. Council provided input on what successful engagement looks like
to them with regards to the City-wide Plan. Council provided further input on how they feel the
City can best engage diverse communities in the City-wide Plan.
* * * * *
MOVED by Councillor De Genova
SECONDED by Councillor Boyle
THAT Council recess for 45 minutes.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY
* * * * *
Council recessed at 12:35 pm and reconvened at 1:20 pm.
* * * * *
Following the recess, Council provided feedback on how they would like to be involved in the
City-wide Plan process going forward.
