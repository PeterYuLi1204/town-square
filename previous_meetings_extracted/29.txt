SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
November 6, 2025
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Thursday,
November 6, 2025, at 4:33 pm, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose
of considering the report entitled “Declaration of Manifest Error – Tax Sale of 1208–1575 West
10th Avenue”, and considering a motion to convene a meeting which is closed to the public.
This Special Council meeting was convened in person and via electronic means as authorized
by Part 14 of the Procedure By-law.

The Acting Mayor acknowledged we are on the unceded homelands of the Musqueam,
Squamish, and Tsleil-Waututh People. We thank them for having cared for this land and look
forward to working with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.

2. Declaration of Manifest Error – Tax Sale of 1208 – 1575 West 10th Avenue
The Assistant Director, Municipal/Governance, Legal Services, provided an introduction.
* * * * *
Subsequently, it was
MOVED by Councillor Kirby-Yung
SECONDED by Councillor Klassen
THAT Council suspend section 7.5(a) of the Procedure By-law in order to allow unlimited
speaking time for the speaker.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY AND
BY THE REQUIRED MAJORITY
* * * * *
Council heard from one speaker who spoke in opposition to the report recommendations.
The Assistant Director, Municipal/Governance, Legal Services, responded to questions.
MOVED by Councillor Klassen
SECONDED by Councillor Zhou
THAT Council hereby cancels the sale to the tax sale purchaser of the registered
leasehold interest in the property known as 1208 - 1575 West 10th Avenue, Vancouver,
BC and legally described as:
Parcel Identifier: 024-230-251
Legal Description:
STRATA LOT 81 DISTRICT LOT 526 GROUP 1 NEW WESTMINSTER
DISTRICT LEASEHOLD STRATA PLAN LMS3443 TOGETHER WITH AN
INTEREST IN THE COMMON PROPERTY IN PROPORTION TO THE UNIT
ENTITLEMENT OF THE STRATA LOT AS SHOWN ON FORM 1,
due to a manifest error in the proceedings leading to the sale in the 2024 Property Tax
Sale and approve the refund to the tax sale purchaser of the upset price plus 6 %
interest in accordance with section 445 of the Vancouver Charter.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY (Vote No. 11127)
(Councillor Meiszner absent for the vote)
