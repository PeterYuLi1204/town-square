SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
June 4, 2024
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Tuesday, June 4, 2024,
at 12:06 pm, in the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of considering a
motion to change the Acting Mayor and Deputy Mayor Roster. This Special Council meeting
was convened in person and via electronic means as authorized by Part 14 of the Procedure
By-law.
PRESENT: Councillor Sarah Kirby-Yung, Acting Mayor
Councillor Christine Boyle
Councillor Pete Fry

Councillor Lenny Zhou
ABSENT: Mayor Ken Sim
Councillor Rebecca Bligh
Councillor Adriane Carr
Councillor Lisa Dominato
Councillor Brian Montague
CITY MANAGER’S OFFICE: Paul Mochrie, City Manager
CITY CLERK’S OFFICE: Katrina Leckovic, City Clerk
Cassia Nasralla, Meeting Coordinator
WELCOME
The Acting Mayor acknowledged we are on the unceded homelands of the Musqueam,
Squamish, and Tsleil-Waututh People. We thank them for having cared for this land and look
forward to working with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
1. Change to the Acting Mayor and Deputy Mayor: Roster of Councillors to
Represent Council - 2024
MOVED by Councillor Meiszner
SECONDED by Councillor Klassen
WHEREAS
1. Council approved the 2024 Acting Mayor Roster at the Council meeting on
December 12, 2023;

Special Council Meeting
Minutes, Tuesday, June 04, 2024 2
2. Councillor Carr was approved as Acting Mayor for the month of June 2024;
3. Councillor Kirby-Yung agreed to act as replacement for Councillor Carr and was
approved as Acting Mayor on May 8, 2024;
4. Councillor Kirby-Yung is away on Civic Business from June 5 to 8, 2024,
inclusive; and
5. Council approved the 2024 Deputy Mayor Roster and Councillor Zhou was
appointed for the month of June; as such, from June 5 to 8, 2024, an alternate
Deputy Mayor is required.
THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED THAT Councillor Zhou replace Councillor Kirby-Yung
as Acting Mayor from June 5 to 8, 2024;
FURTHER THAT Councillor Boyle be appointed as Deputy Mayor from June 5 to 8,
2024.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY (Vote No. 10023)
ADJOURNMENT
MOVED by Councillor Fry
SECONDED by Councillor Meiszner
THAT the meeting be adjourned.
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY
The Special Council adjourned at 12:15 pm.
