SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING MINUTES
SEPTEMBER 28, 2021
A Special Meeting of the Council of the City of Vancouver was held on Tuesday, September 28, 2021,
at 6 pm, in the Council Chambers, Third Floor, City Hall, for the purpose of engaging Council in a
workshop to discuss Vancouver Plan and hear from relevant experts. This meeting was convened by
electronic means as authorized under the Order of the Minister of Public Safety and Solicitor General of
the Province of British Columbia – Emergency Program Act, updated Ministerial Order No. M192.

WELCOME
The Deputy Mayor acknowledged we are on the unceded territories of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh Nations and we thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to working
with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Deputy Mayor also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s staff who work
hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and play.

Special Council Meeting
Minutes, Tuesday, September 28, 2021 2
1. Vancouver Plan Panelist Discussion
Diana Bulley, President and Founder of IdeaSpace, and Facilitator for the discussion, provided opening
remarks, followed by presentations from Sean Galloway, Director of Regional Planning and Electoral
Area Services at Metro Vancouver, and City staff, Susan Haid, Deputy Director, Long Range and
Strategic Planning.
Following the introductory presentations, Diana provided a brief overview of the workshop and
introduced the following panelists, who each provided a presentation.
• Mary W. Rowe, President & CEO, Canadian Urban Institute
• Marc Lee, Senior Economist, Canadian Centre for Policy Alternatives
• Dr. John Rose, Instructor, Department of Geography and Environment, Kwantlen Polytechnic
University
• Andrew Ramlo, Executive Director, Urban Futures; and, Vice President, Intelligence, Rennie
Group
• Dr. Julia Harten, Assistant Professor, School of Community and Regional Planning, UBC
* * * * *
Following the panelist presentations, the meeting recessed at 8:10 pm, and reconvened at 8:15 pm.
* * * * *
Following the recess, Diana conducted a roundtable discussion building on themes heard during the
question period. Following the roundtable, the panelists and facilitator made concluding remarks.
