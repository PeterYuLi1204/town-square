MAYOR’S BUDGET TASK FORCE IMPLEMENTATION
OVERSIGHT COMMITTEE

A Meeting of the Mayor’s Budget Task Force Implementation Oversight Committee was held on
Tuesday, July 30, 2024, at 1:00 PM, in the Cascadia Room, Third Floor, City Hall. This
Committee meeting was convened in person and via electronic means as authorized by Part 14
of the Procedure By-law.

WELCOME
The Chair acknowledged we are on the unceded homelands of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh People. We thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to working
with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
REPORTS
1. Reprioritizing the Structure of the Finance Committee
The Deputy City Manager introduced the item and responded to questions and comments.
2. City of Vancouver’s Core Jurisdiction
The Deputy City Manager, along with City Clerk’s Office staff, introduced the item and
responded to questions and comments.
The Committee agreed, by consensus, to request that staff present a jurisdictional scan of
comparable North American municipalities’ core jurisdictions, at the October 15, 2024
Committee meeting.

3. Other Works in Progress
The Deputy City Manager, along with City Clerk’s Office staff, introduced the item and
responded to questions and comments.
The Committee agreed, by consensus, to request that staff present a report on work in progress
on Budget Task Force recommendations, at the October 15, 2024 Committee meeting.
4. Fall Meeting Schedule
City Clerk’s Office staff introduced the item and responded to questions and comments.
The Committee agreed, by consensus, to convene monthly, beginning October 15, 2024.
The Committee agreed, by consensus, to request that staff present a Council transmittal report
on the Committee’s work, for consideration at the October 15, 2024 Committee meeting.
