REPORT TO COUNCIL
AUDITOR GENERAL COMMITTEE MINUTES
April 4, 2024
A Meeting of the Auditor General Committee was held on Thursday, April 4, 2024, at 9:31 am, in
the Council Chamber, Third Floor, City Hall. This Committee meeting was convened in person
and via electronic means as authorized by Part 14 of the Procedure By-law.

WELCOME
The Chair acknowledged we are on the unceded homelands of the Musqueam, Squamish, and
Tsleil-Waututh People. We thank them for having cared for this land and look forward to working
with them in partnership as we continue to build this great city together.
The Chair also recognized the immense contributions of the City of Vancouver’s staff who work
hard every day to help make our city an incredible place to live, work, and play.
