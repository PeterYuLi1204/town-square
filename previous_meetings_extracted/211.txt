2. Potential Finance Committee Structures
The City Manager, along with City Clerk’s Office staff, introduced the additional written report
and responded to questions and comments.
The Committee agreed, by consensus, to request that staff present a jurisdictional scan of
comparable Canadian municipalities’ Finance and Budget Committees, and projections for staff
resources required for a Finance Standing Committee, at the July 16, 2024 Committee meeting.
The Committee agreed, by consensus, to request that staff present a status report on all
Mayor’s Budget Task Force recommendations, by year end.
